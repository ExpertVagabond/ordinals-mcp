24 Tools

Inscriptions

  • get_inscriptionMetadata by ID or number
  • search_inscriptionsFilter by address, mime, rarity, block
  • get_inscription_contentRaw content (text, images)
  • get_inscription_transfersFull transfer history
  • get_inscription_traitsCollection trait info

Address

  • get_address_inscriptionsAll inscriptions for address
  • get_brc20_balancesBRC-20 token balances
  • get_rune_balancesRune balances
  • get_address_rare_satsRare satoshis held

Runes

  • get_rune_infoEtching details, supply, terms
  • list_runesPaginated rune listing
  • get_rune_holdersTop holders by balance
  • get_rune_activityMints, transfers, burns
  • get_rune_market_infoPrice, market cap, volume
  • get_rune_unlock_dateName availability check

BRC-20

  • get_brc20_tokenToken details & supply
  • get_brc20_activityDeploy, mint, transfer events
  • get_brc20_holdersTop token holders

Collections & Market

  • get_collection_infoStats, floor, volume
  • get_collection_inscriptionsInscriptions in collection
  • get_collection_listingsMagic Eden listings

Sats & Transactions

  • get_sat_infoRarity, epoch, inscriptions
  • get_tx_inscriptionsInscriptions in a TX
  • get_tx_runesRune transfers in a TX
